Foreground Focus: Enhancing Coherence and Fidelity in Camouflaged Image Generation
Pei-Chi Chen, Yi Yao, Chan-Feng Hsu, HongXia Xie, Hung-Jen Chen, Hong-Han Shuai, Wen-Huang Cheng
ICME, 2025

We propose a camouflaged image generation framework that enhances foreground-background harmonization while preserving foreground fidelity. Our method achieves state-of-the-art results on multiple benchmarks.
